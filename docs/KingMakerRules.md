Kingdom StatisticsThe four primary statistics govern the kingdom's health and ability to succeed in events.Culture: The kingdom’s academic and artistic standing. Influences the ability to resist outside influence.Economy: The kingdom’s trade, wealth, and efficiency. Affects income and building speed.Loyalty: The citizens’ devotion.
