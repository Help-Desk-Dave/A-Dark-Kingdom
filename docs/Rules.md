A DARK KINGDOM: OFFICIAL RULEBOOK

1.0 INTRODUCTION & OBJECTIVE

Welcome to the Stolen Lands. You have arrived in a dangerous, untamed wilderness—a vast swamp of mangroves, biting insects, and ancient ruins.

A Dark Kingdom is a real-time survival and kingdom management game. You begin with nothing but the clothes on your back and a few sticks for a fire. As you survive the elements and attract wanderers, your campsite will grow into a burgeoning settlement, and eventually, a sprawling barony.

Objective: Survive the early days of the wilderness, unfurl your Royal Charter, and expand your kingdom across the 10x10 hex map without letting your treasury (BP) run dry or your citizens fall to Unrest.

2.0 COMPONENTS (THE UI)

Your dashboard is divided into three primary zones:

The Map (Center): Initially shows your local 5x5 settlement grid. Later, it expands to show the 10x10 World Map.

The Ledger (Right): Tracks your vital statistics: Build Points (BP), Unrest, Experience (XP), and your roster of Advisors and prominent citizens.

The Event Log (Bottom): A real-time chronological readout of everything happening in the swamp. Pay attention to this; it is how the game speaks to you.

3.0 SETUP (THE AWAKENING)

The game handles setup automatically via the "Dark Room" prologue.

Gather: You must manually gather sticks and build a fire.

Survive: Hunt for rations and gather timber.

Camp: Establish your first settlement.

Automate: Build Pioneer Tents to attract wanderers, then assign them to gather resources for you.

Once you have 5 citizens, you may Sign the Charter and officially begin the Kingdom Phase.

4.0 THE GAME TICK (TURN STRUCTURE)

Unlike traditional board games, A Dark Kingdom operates in real-time.

The Tick: Every 5 real-world seconds, the game "ticks" forward by one month.

Production & Consumption: During a tick, assigned workers produce resources, and your population consumes Rations.

The Annual Upkeep: Every 12 ticks (1 minute of real time), an entire year passes. You must pay the Crown an Annual Upkeep of 25 BP.

5.0 ACTIONS

Once you are in the Kingdom Phase (Stage 4), you spend BP to take actions on the World Map.

Reconnoiter (Cost: 5 BP): Select an unexplored (??) hex. This reveals the terrain type and any hidden resources.

Claim Hex (Cost: 10 BP): Select a reconnoitered hex. You annex it into your kingdom, increasing your borders and allowing you to build new settlements.

Build Structure (Cost: Varies): Inside a claimed settlement, you may construct buildings (Taverns, Academies, Piers) to increase your housing capacity, generate BP, or lower Unrest.

6.0 THE ECONOMY & ADVISORS

Build Points (BP) are the lifeblood of your kingdom.

If you attempt to buy something that drops your treasury below 15 BP, your Treasurer will issue a strict warning.

If you fail to pay your 25 BP Annual Upkeep, your kingdom will fall into debt, causing Unrest to spike.

Advisors: You can assign skilled companions to roles like Treasurer or General. A Treasurer grants bonus BP every tick based on their Attribute score.

7.0 WINNING & LOSING

There is no final victory condition—the goal is endless survival and expansion across the Stolen Lands.

Losing: If your Unrest grows too high due to starvation or insurmountable debt, the citizens will riot, and the Stolen Lands will reclaim your kingdom.
